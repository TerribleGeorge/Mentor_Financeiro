**Package Android:** `com.georgeguimares.mentorfinanceiro`

This Privacy Policy clearly explains what data Mentor Financeiro can access, collect, use, store, share and protect. It is written to reflect the current operation of the application and should be read in conjunction with the Terms of Use and Security page.

Mentor Financeiro is an application for financial organization, financial education, goals, expense records, graphs, reports, investment content and optional Premium subscription features. ## 2. Summary in simple language

- The app helps you organize expenses, goals, limits, financial history, investments and educational content.
- To use certain features, you can log in with email/password, Google account, Apple account or incognito mode, through Firebase Authentication.
- The financial data you provide, such as expenses, goals, limits, profile and preferences, can be saved in Firebase/Cloud Firestore for synchronization and account operation.
- The app uses Google/Firebase services for login, database, push notifications, analytics and fault diagnosis.
- The app may use Google AdMob to display ads.
- Premium is processed by Google Play Billing within the app itself.
- Location is optional and used only when you give permission, for example to adapt investment comparisons to the country or local context.
- The notification monitoring feature is optional. If you enable notification access on the Android, the app can read the title and text of supported notifications to identify purchases, payments, and transfers and turn these into financial records. The app does not access your bank account and does not ask for a password.
- Images chosen by you for personalization may be stored locally on the device.
- You can request account and data deletion via the email indicated in this policy.

## 3. Data we may process

Depending on how you use the application, we may process the following categories of data.

### 3.1 Account and identification data

- Name provided in the registration.
- E-mail.
- Firebase (UID) user identifier.
- Login method used.
- Profile photo, when provided by the login provider.
- Premium subscription state associated with the profile.
- Technical data necessary to maintain the session and synchronize information.

### 3.2 Financial data entered by you

- Expenses, expenses, income, categories, financial history and transactions.
- Goals, limits, planning, financial dreams and preferences.
- Information used in graphs, reports, analyzes and automatic mentoring within the app.
- Investor profile, objectives and answers provided in questionnaires or planning screens.

This data can be sensitive in a practical sense, because it reveals aspects of your financial life. Therefore, you should only register information that you want to keep in the application.

### 3.3 Subscription and purchase data

- Purchased product, subscription status, period, renewal, purchase restoration and technical identifiers required to confirm Premium.
- Purchase, cancellation, renewal, refund and billing are processed by Google Play, in accordance with Google account and store rules.

The app does not collect or store card numbers, card security codes or bank payment details.

### 3.4 Usage, diagnostic and performance data

- Usage and interaction events in the app, for example screens accessed, resources used and flows completed.
- Aggregate or technical data to understand product stability, performance, and improvement.
- Crash reports and diagnostics by Firebase Crashlytics, where applicable, including device technical information, app version, operating system, error logs, and user identifier when configured.

### 3.5 Push notification data

If you allow notifications from the app, we can handle:

- Firebase Cloud Messaging token (FCM).
- Preferences related to receiving notifications.
- Topics or segments necessary for sending communications from the application itself.

You can control whether notifications are allowed in the Android settings.

### 3.6 Notification data read in Android

Notification monitoring is an optional feature. It only works if you manually enable notification access in the Android settings.

When enabled, the app can receive the title, text, source app package name, time, and technical metadata from notifications displayed on your device. The objective is to identify notifications compatible with expenses, purchases, payments, subscriptions and transfers to generate financial records.

Important measures of this feature:

- The app does not access your bank account.
- The app does not log into banks, cards or digital wallets.
- The app does not ask for a banking password.
- The app applies filters to avoid capturing security codes, OTP, passwords and authentication messages.
- The focus of the filter is to identify compatible purchases, payments, transfers sent and financial movements.
- You can disable the feature in the app or revoke permission in the Android settings.

Even with filters, notifications can contain sensitive information. Only enable this feature if you understand and accept this type of processing.

### 3.7 Location

The app can request approximate or precise location, according to the permission granted by Android, to adapt comparisons, content or investment context to the country/region.

Location is not required for all features. If you deny permission, some comparisons or customizations may be limited.

### 3.8 Local images and files

When you choose an image to visually customize the app, it can be stored locally on the device. Unless specifically implemented in the future, these personalization images are not sent to our servers.

### 3.9 Advertising

The app may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may process device identifiers, usage data, technical information and advertising signals in accordance with Google's own policies and the user's ad settings.

### 3.10 Data from external market APIs

The app can consult external APIs for exchange rates, quotes, indices or market information. These queries typically involve parameters such as currency, asset, country or symbol being searched. It is not necessary to send your registered personal financial data to obtain this information.

## 9. Security

We adopt reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including:

- Authentication by Firebase.
- Access rules in the database to limit data to the corresponding user.
- Communication encrypted by the SDKs and APIs used.
- Use of Android permissions only when related to app resources.

No system is 100% secure. You should also protect your device, use a screen lock, keep a strong password, avoid sharing your account and update the app when a new version is released.
