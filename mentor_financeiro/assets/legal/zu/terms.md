# Imigomo Yokusebenzisa - Mentor Financeiro

**Igcine ukubuyekezwa:** Meyi 19, 2026

Le Migomo Yokusebenzisa ilawula ukufinyelela nokusetshenziswa kohlelo lokusebenza Mentor Financeiro. Ngokufaka, ukufinyelela noma ukusebenzisa uhlelo lokusebenza, uqinisekisa ukuthi ufundile, waqonda futhi uyavumelana nale migomo, Inqubomgomo Yobumfihlo kanye nolwazi Lwezokuvikela.

Uma ungavumelani, ungasebenzisi uhlelo lokusebenza.

## 1. Yini Mentor Financeiro

Mentor Financeiro isicelo senhlangano yomuntu siqu yezezimali, imfundo yezezimali, amarekhodi ezindleko, imigomo, imikhawulo, umlando, amagrafu, imibiko, okuqukethwe kokutshalwa kwezimali kanye nezinsiza Premium.

Uhlelo lokusebenza lunezinjongo zolwazi, zemfundo kanye nenhlangano.

Mentor Financeiro akuyona:

- ibhange;
- broker;
- umeluleki wokutshalwa kwezimali obhalisiwe;
- umhleli wezezimali oqinisekisiwe;
- inkampani yomshuwalense;
- indlela yokukhokha;
- isikhungo sezikweletu;
- inkundla yokwenza imiyalo yokutshala imali.

## 2. Ukusetshenziswa kwezemfundo nokuntuleka kokunconywa komuntu siqu

Okuqukethwe, ukulingisa, izexwayiso, amagrafu, iziqhathaniso, iziphakamiso, imibhalo yokuqondisa kanye nolwazi lokutshalwa kwezimali kunikezwa imfundo kanye nenhlangano.

Azihlanganisi izincomo zokutshala izimali zomuntu ngamunye, izithembiso zenzuzo, umthetho osemthethweni, i-accounting, iseluleko sentela noma sezezimali.

Ngaphambi kokwenza izinqumo zezezimali, ukuthenga imikhiqizo, ukutshala imali, ukuhlenga, ukuxhasa ngezimali, ukuboleka noma ukuthatha ubungozi, hlola isimo sakho somuntu siqu futhi, uma kunesidingo, thintana nochwepheshe abaqeqeshiwe.

## 3. Ukubhalisa kanye ne-akhawunti

Ezinye izici zingase zidinge ukungena ngemvume nge-imeyili/iphasiwedi, i-Google, i-Apple, noma imodi ye-incognito.

Unesibopho salokhu:

- nikeza ulwazi olunembile;
- gcina ukufinyelela okuphephile ku-imeyili yakho kanye nedivayisi;
- vikela iphasiwedi yakho ne-akhawunti;
- ungabelani ngemininingwane;
- xwayisa ngokusetshenziswa kabi okusolwayo.

Singakhawulela, simise okwesikhashana noma sinqamule ukufinyelela uma kukhona ukuhlukumeza, ukukhwabanisa, ukwephulwa kwale migomo, ubungozi bokuphepha noma isidingo esingokomthetho.

## 4. Idatha efakwe nguwe

Unesibopho sedatha oyibhalisa kuhlelo lokusebenza, okuhlanganisa izindleko, imigomo, imikhawulo, umlando, ulwazi lwephrofayela kanye nezintandokazi.

Uhlelo lokusebenza lungahlela, lucubungule, lubonise, luvumelanise futhi luhlaziye le datha ukuze ilethe amakhono ayo.

Kufanele ubuyekeze amarekhodi, ikakhulukazi idatha engenisiwe noma ekhonjwe ngokuzenzakalelayo ngezaziso, ngaphambi kokwenza izinqumo zezezimali.

## 5. Ukuqapha Izaziso

Uhlelo lokusebenza lungase lunikeze isici Android sokulandelela isaziso esingakhethwa ukuze sihlonze ukusetshenziswa kwemali, ukuthenga, izinkokhelo, okubhaliselwe, nokudluliswa kwezaziso ezisekelwe.

Lesi sici:

- kuncike kwimvume enikezwa nguwe ku Android;
- ingavalwa nganoma yisiphi isikhathi;
- ayingeni ku-akhawunti yasebhange;
- ayiceli iphasiwedi yasebhange;
- ayisusi imali;
- isebenzisa izihlungi ukugwema OTP amakhodi, amagama ayimfihlo kanye nemiyalezo yokuphepha;
- angenza amaphutha, ahluleke ukuhlonza ukuthengiselana noma ukuhlukanisa imininingwane ngokungeyikho.

Unesibopho sokubuyekeza nokulungisa amarekhodi ngokuzenzakalelayo lapho kudingeka.

## 6. Ukubhalisa Premium

Mentor Financeiro ingase inikeze Premium izici ngokubhalisa noma ukuthenga ngaphakathi nohlelo.

Ukuthenga kucutshungulwa yi-Google Play. Izintengo, izintela, isikhathi sesivivinyo, ukuvuselela, ukukhansela, imbuyiselo kanye nendlela yokukhokha kulandela imithetho eboniswe ku-Google Play kanye ne-akhawunti yakho ye-Google.

Uma kunesikhathi samahhala noma sokuphromotha, ukukhokha kungase kuqhubeke ngokuzenzakalelayo ekupheleni kwesikhathi, ngaphandle kwalapho kukhanselwe phakathi nesikhathi esichazwe ngu-Google Play.

Ukukhansela okubhaliselwe kuvimbela izinkokhiso zesikhathi esizayo, kodwa akukhiqizi imali mbuyiselo ngokuzenzakalelayo ezikhathini esezikhokhisiwe kakade, ngaphandle kwemithetho ye-Google Play noma umthetho osebenzayo.

Premium izinsiza zingashintsha, zilungiswe, zisuswe noma zishintshwe, kuncike kumalungelo asebenzayo.

## 7. Izikhangiso

Uhlelo lokusebenza lungabonisa izikhangiso. Izikhangiso zinganikezwa izinkampani zangaphandle, ezifana ne-Google AdMob, kuye ngezinqubomgomo zabo kanye nezilungiselelo ze-akhawunti/idivayisi yakho.

Asinasibopho ngemikhiqizo, amasevisi, iminikelo noma okuqukethwe okuvela kubakhangisi bangaphandle.

## 8. Ukusetshenziswa okwamukelekayo

Uyavuma ukuthi:- sebenzisa uhlelo lokusebenza ukukhwabanisa, ukuhlukumeza, ukuxhaphaza imali, ukwephula umthetho noma ukulimaza abantu besithathu;
- zama ukufinyelela idatha kwabanye abasebenzisi;
- ukuphepha kwe-bypass, imikhawulo, ukubhalisa, i-paywall noma izindlela zobuchwepheshe;
- unjiniyela wokuhlehlisa, kopisha, uguqule noma usabalalise kabusha uhlelo lokusebenza ngaphandle kokugunyazwa;
- sebenzisa ngokuzenzakalelayo ukusetshenziswa kabi, izinsizakalo ezilayisha ngokweqile noma ziphazamise ukusebenza kwazo;
- faka okuqukethwe okungekho emthethweni, okuhlaselayo, okudukisayo noma okwephula amalungelo abantu besithathu.

## 9. Izinkonzo zezinkampani zangaphandle

Uhlelo lokusebenza luncike ezinsizeni zezinkampani zangaphandle ezifana ne-Firebase, Google Play, i-AdMob, i-marketplace APIs, Android uhlelo, nabahlinzeki bokungena ngemvume.

Lawa masevisi angase abe nemigomo yawo, izinqubomgomo, ukungatholakali, imikhawulo nezinqubo. Ukusebenza kohlelo lokusebenza kungase kuthintwa izinguquko noma ukwehluleka kulawa masevisi.

## 10. Ukutholakala nezinguquko

Sifuna ukugcina uhlelo lokusebenza lusebenza kahle, kodwa asiqinisekisi ukutholakala okuqhubekayo, ukungabi khona kwamaphutha, ukusebenzisana nawo wonke amadivayisi noma ukugcinwa unomphela kwazo zonke izinsiza.

Singase sibuyekeze, silungise, sisuse, sishintshe noma simise izici, ikakhulukazi ngenxa yezizathu zobuchwepheshe, zomthetho, zokuphepha, zebhizinisi noma zokuthuthukisa umkhiqizo.

## 11. Impahla yobuhlakani

Igama Mentor Financeiro, isixhumi esibonakalayo, imibhalo, inhlangano, ikhodi, izici ezibonakalayo, izinsiza nokuqukethwe kohlelo lokusebenza kungokanjiniyela noma abanikezeli bamalayisense, ngaphandle kokuqukethwe kwenkampani yangaphandle.

Ukusetshenziswa kohlelo lokusebenza akudluliseli impahla yengqondo kuwe.

## 12. Umkhawulo wesikweletu

Ngokwezinga eligcwele elivunyelwe umthetho, i-Mentor Financeiro ayinasibopho ngalokhu:

- izinqumo zezezimali ezenziwe ngokusekelwe kuhlelo lokusebenza;
- ukulahlekelwa, ukulahlekelwa, inzuzo elahlekile noma ukulahleka kwamathuba;
- amaphutha edatha afakwe ngumsebenzisi;
- ukuhlukaniswa okuzenzakalelayo okungalungile;
- ukwehluleka kwe-inthanethi, Firebase, Google Play, Android, ama-API noma abantu besithathu;
- ukungatholakali, ukulahlekelwa idatha yendawo, izinkinga zedivayisi noma imvume echithiwe;
- imiphumela yokutshalwa kwezimali, inzuzo, izintela, izimali noma izimo zemakethe.

Akukho lutho kule migomo okufaka ngaphandle amalungelo angenakunqunyelwa umthetho.

## 13. Ukuvala

Ungayeka ukusebenzisa uhlelo lokusebenza noma kunini, ususe idatha yendawo, ucele ukususwa kwedatha yefu futhi ukhansele okubhaliselwe ku-Google Play.

Ukususa uhlelo lokusebenza kudivayisi akukhanseli ngokuzenzakalela ukubhalisa okusebenzayo. Ukuze ukhansele izinkokhelo zesikhathi esizayo, sebenzisa Google Play.

## 14. Ubumfihlo nokuvikeleka

Ukusetshenziswa kwedatha kuchazwe kuNqubomgomo Yobumfihlo. Izinqubo zokuphepha zichazwe ekhasini Lezokuphepha.

Ngokusebenzisa uhlelo lokusebenza, uyavuma ukuthi idatha yezezimali ingase ibe bucayi futhi kufanele ugcine idivayisi yakho ivikelekile.

## 15. Izinguquko kule migomo

Singase sibuyekeze le Migomo Yokusebenzisa ukuze sibonise izinguquko kuhlelo lokusebenza, izici, okubhaliselwe, izimfuneko zomthetho, noma amasevisi ezinkampani zangaphandle.

Idethi ephezulu ibonisa inguqulo yamanje. Ukusetshenziswa okuqhubekayo kohlelo lokusebenza ngemva kwezinguquko kusho isivumelwano nenguqulo ebuyekeziwe, uma kuvunyelwe ngokomthetho.
