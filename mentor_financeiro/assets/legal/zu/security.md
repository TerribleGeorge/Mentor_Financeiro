Leli khasi lichaza Mentor Financeiro izinqubo zokuphepha ngolimi oluqondile. Ihambisana neNqubomgomo Yobumfihlo kanye Nemibandela Yokusebenzisa.

## 1. Ukuzibophezela Kwezokuphepha

Mentor Financeiro yasungulwa ukuze isize ngenhlangano yezezimali ngaphandle kokucela ukufinyelela okuqondile ku-akhawunti yasebhange, ngaphandle kokucela iphasiwedi yasebhange futhi ngaphandle kokusebenza njengebhange, umdayisi noma indlela yokukhokha.

Uhlelo lokusebenza lungagcina idatha yezezimali efakwe nguwe noma ekhonjwe ngezici ozikhethela zona, yingakho siphatha ukuphepha njengengxenye emaphakathi yomkhiqizo.

## 2. Lokho uhlelo lokusebenza olungakwenzi

- Ayiceli iphasiwedi yasebhange, ikhadi noma isikhwama semali yedijithali.
- Ayifinyeleli ku-akhawunti yakho yasebhange.
- Ayisusi imali.
- Ayenzi izinkokhelo, i-Pix, ukudluliselwa noma ukutshala imali egameni lakho.
- Ayiceli ikhodi yokuphepha yekhadi.
- Ayiceli ikhodi yasebhange OTP.
- Ayithengisi idatha yakho yomuntu siqu.

## 3. Izinyathelo zobuchwepheshe ezisetshenzisiwe

Ngokuya ngesisetshenziswa esisetshenzisiwe, uhlelo lokusebenza lungasebenzisa:

- Ngena ngemvume nge-Firebase Authentication.
- Cloud Firestore enedatha ehlotshaniswa nomsebenzisi oqinisekisiwe.
- Imithetho yokuphepha yokukhawulela ukufinyelela kudatha yomsebenzisi siqu.
- Ukuxhumana kubethelwe ama-SDK namasevisi asetshenziswa.
- Firebase Crashlytics ukuhlonza amaphutha ezobuchwepheshe nokulungisa izinkinga.
- Android izimvume ezicelwe kuphela uma zihlobene nezici zohlelo lokusebenza.
- Isitoreji sasendaweni sezintandokazi, itimu, inqolobane nedatha yedivayisi.

## 4. Ukuqapha Izaziso

Ukuqapha isaziso kuyinketho futhi kudinga ukuthi kusebenze mathupha ku-Android.

Uma inikwe amandla, uhlelo lokusebenza lungafunda izaziso ezisekelwayo ukuze libone ukusetshenziswa kwemali, ukuthenga, izinkokhelo, okubhaliselwe, nokudluliselwa. Umgomo uwukunciphisa umsebenzi wezandla lapho uqopha izindleko.

Ukunciphisa ubungozi:

- Uhlelo lokusebenza likwazisa ngesizathu semvume ngaphambi kokukuqondisa kuzilungiselelo.
- Ukufinyelela kungahoxiswa noma nini kuzilungiselelo Android.
- Isihlungi sicinga imicimbi yezezimali ehambisanayo, hhayi izingxoxo zomuntu siqu.
- Uhlelo lokusebenza luzama ukuziba OTP amakhodi, amagama ayimfihlo, amakhodi okuphepha nemilayezo yokuqinisekisa.
- Uhlelo lokusebenza alusebenzisi lesi sici ukufinyelela amabhange, amakhadi noma izikhwama zedijithali.

Noma kunjalo, izaziso zingaqukatha idatha ebucayi. Sebenzisa lesi sici kuphela uma uvumelana nalolu hlobo lokucubungula.

Uhlelo lokusebenza lungacela:

- **I-inthanethi:** ukuxhuma ku-Firebase, Google Play, ama-API nezinsiza eziku-inthanethi.

- **Indawo:** ukulungiswa kokuqukethwe noma ukuqhathanisa nezwe/isifunda, uma kugunyaziwe.
- **Ukufinyelela Android izaziso:** umazisi ozikhethela wona wezindleko ezivela kuzaziso ezihambisanayo.

Ulawula lezi zimvume kuzilungiselelo ze-Android.
