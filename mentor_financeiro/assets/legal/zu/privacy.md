## 3. Idatha esingase siyicubungule

Kuya ngokuthi ulusebenzisa kanjani uhlelo lokusebenza, singase sicubungule izigaba ezilandelayo zedatha.

### 3.1 I-akhawunti nedatha yokuhlonza

- Igama linikezwe ekubhaliseni.
- I-imeyili.
- Firebase (UID) isihlonzi somsebenzisi.
- Indlela yokungena isetshenzisiwe.
- Isithombe sephrofayili, lapho sinikezwa umhlinzeki wokungena.
- Premium isimo sokubhalisa esihlotshaniswa nephrofayela.
- Idatha yobuchwepheshe edingekayo ukuze kugcinwe isikhathi nokuvumelanisa ulwazi.

### 3.2 Idatha yezezimali efakwe nguwe

- Izindleko, izindleko, imali engenayo, izigaba, umlando wezezimali kanye nokuthengiselana.
- Imigomo, imikhawulo, ukuhlela, amaphupho ezezimali kanye nezintandokazi.
- Ulwazi olusetshenziswa emagrafu, imibiko, ukuhlaziya kanye nokuqondisa okuzenzakalelayo ngaphakathi kohlelo lokusebenza.
- Iphrofayili yabatshalizimali, izinhloso nezimpendulo ezinikezwe kuhlu lwemibuzo noma ezikrinini zokuhlela.

Le datha ingaba bucayi ngomqondo ongokoqobo, ngoba iveza izici zempilo yakho yezezimali. Ngakho-ke, kufanele ubhalise kuphela imininingwane ofuna ukuyigcina esicelweni.

### 3.3 Idatha yokubhalisa nokuthenga

- Umkhiqizo othengiwe, isimo sokubhaliselwe, isikhathi, ukuvuselelwa, ukubuyiselwa kokuthenga nezihlonzi zobuchwepheshe ezidingekayo ukuze kuqinisekiswe Premium.
- Ukuthenga, ukukhansela, ukuvuselela, ukubuyiselwa kwemali nezinkokhiso kucutshungulwa yi-Google Play, ngokuvumelana ne-akhawunti ye-Google nemithetho yesitolo.

Uhlelo lokusebenza aluqoqi noma alugcini izinombolo zekhadi, amakhodi okuphepha ekhadi noma imininingwane yokukhokha yasebhange.

### 3.4 Ukusetshenziswa, ukuxilonga nokusebenza kwedatha- Ukusetshenziswa nemicimbi yokusebenzelana kuhlelo lokusebenza, isibonelo izikrini ezifinyelelwe, izinsiza ezisetshenzisiwe nokugeleza kuqediwe.
- Idatha ehlanganisiwe noma yobuchwepheshe ukuze uqonde ukuzinza, ukusebenza nokuthuthukiswa komkhiqizo.
- Imibiko yephutha nokuxilongwa nge-Firebase Crashlytics, uma kusebenza, okuhlanganisa ulwazi lobuchwepheshe bedivayisi, inguqulo yohlelo lokusebenza, isistimu yokusebenza, amalogi wamaphutha nesihlonzi somsebenzisi uma silungiswa.

### 3.5 Idatha yesaziso sohlelo lokusebenza

Uma uvumela izaziso ezivela kuhlelo lokusebenza, singakwazi ukusingatha:

- Firebase Cloud Messaging ithokheni (FCM).
- Izintandokazi ezihlobene nokwamukela izaziso.
- Izihloko noma izingxenye ezidingekayo ukuze kuthunyelwe ukuxhumana kusuka kuhlelo lokusebenza ngokwalo.

Ungakwazi ukulawula ukuthi izaziso zivunyelwe yini ku-Android izilungiselelo.

### 3.6 Idatha yezaziso ifundwe ku-Android

Ukuqapha isaziso kuyisici ozikhethela sona. Isebenza kuphela uma wena unika amandla ukufinyelela kwesaziso kuzilungiselelo Android.

Uma inikwe amandla, uhlelo lokusebenza lungathola isihloko, umbhalo, igama lephakheji yohlelo lokusebenza lomthombo, isikhathi, kanye nemethadatha yobuchwepheshe evela kuzaziso eziboniswa kudivayisi yakho. Inhloso ukuhlonza izaziso ezihambisana nezindleko, okuthengiwe, izinkokhelo, okubhaliselwe nokudluliswa kwemali ukuze kwenziwe amarekhodi ezezimali.

Izinyathelo ezibalulekile zalesi sici:

- Uhlelo lokusebenza alufinyeleli ku-akhawunti yakho yasebhange.
- Uhlelo lokusebenza alungeni emabhange, amakhadi noma izikhwama zedijithali.
- Uhlelo lokusebenza aluceli iphasiwedi yasebhange.
- Uhlelo lokusebenza lusebenza izihlungi ukugwema ukuthwebula amakhodi okuphepha, OTP, amaphasiwedi nemilayezo yokuqinisekisa.
- Ukugxila kwesihlungi ukuhlonza ukuthenga okuhambisanayo, izinkokhelo, ukudluliselwa okuthunyelwe kanye nomnyakazo wezezimali.
- Ungakhubaza isici kuhlelo lokusebenza noma uhoxise imvume kuzilungiselelo ze-Android.

Ngisho nezihlungi, izaziso zingaqukatha ulwazi olubucayi. Nika lesi sici amandla kuphela uma uqonda futhi wamukela lolu hlobo lokucubungula.

### 3.7 Indawo

Uhlelo lokusebenza lungacela indawo elinganiselwe noma enembile, ngokuya ngemvume enikezwe i-Android, ukuze ivumelanise ukuqhathanisa, okuqukethwe noma umongo wokutshalwa kwezimali ezweni/isifunda.

Indawo ayidingeki kuzo zonke izici. Uma unqabela imvume, okunye ukuqhathanisa noma ukwenza ngezifiso kungase kukhawulelwe.

### 3.8 Izithombe namafayela wendawo

Uma ukhetha isithombe ukuze wenze ngendlela oyifisayo uhlelo lokusebenza, singagcinwa endaweni kudivayisi. Ngaphandle kwalapho zisetshenziswa ngokuqondile esikhathini esizayo, lezi zithombe eziqondene nawe azithunyelwa eziphakelini zethu.

### 3.9 Ukukhangisa

Uhlelo lokusebenza lungabonisa izikhangiso nge-Google AdMob. I-Google ingase icubungule izihlonzi zedivayisi, idatha yokusetshenziswa, ulwazi lobuchwepheshe namasignali okukhangisa ngokuvumelana nezinqubomgomo ze-Google kanye nezilungiselelo zesikhangiso somsebenzisi.

### 3.10 Idatha evela kuma-API emakethe yangaphandle

Uhlelo lokusebenza lungathintana nama-API angaphandle ukuze uthole amanani okushintshanisa, izingcaphuno, izinkomba noma ulwazi lwemakethe. Le mibuzo ivamise ukubandakanya amapharamitha afana nemali, impahla, izwe noma uphawu oluseshwayo. Akudingekile ukuthumela idatha yakho yomuntu siqu ebhalisiwe ukuze uthole lolu lwazi.

- **Android amasevisi esistimu**: izimvume, izaziso nezisetshenziswa zedivayisi.

Lezi zinkampani zangaphandle zingacubungula idatha ngokuvumelana nemigomo yazo, izinqubomgomo nezilungiselelo.
